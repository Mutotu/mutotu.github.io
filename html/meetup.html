About This Page

On this page I would like to show the meeting I attend at CTI- Accelerate-101 program Computer Talent Initiative It is a year long program where we are trained for interviews for computer programming world. Their trainings consist of teaching a programming language, how to prepeare for the interviews, problem solving techniques and also social skills.

The Features Of The Program

CTI program is designed for students that are new to the programming world. Students learn how the programming world works and how to be succeful learning to stay motivated and persistent by having them even learn neuro placticity, for which why they even use dedicated programs where students learn that they are not limited in learning as long as they keep practicing intentionally. Students start learning and practicing from scracth And they bulid from there. CTI program is on a canvas platform where student keep track of the lectures.

Every three weeks there is a general meeting that's held for 3-4 hours where a guest speaker from tech companies or from universities is hosted from that students get to ask questions related to programming world.On top of that all students are divided into smaller group where they need to meet up every week to check in on how they progress on canvas where they study and ask questions to each other under the supervison of a group leaders.

This is a great opportunity for newbees in this programming world. The staff is very helpful and eager to help you along your journey.
